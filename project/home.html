Welcome to my interactive resume!

During my career, I have worked in different positions such as developer, system and business analyst, development manager, solution consultant, presales manager and product manager. Over the course of my career, I have developed an interesting skill set. Working at a critical mission software factory, I developed abilities to prioritize and execute tasks under pressure and keep the engagement of the team. As business analyst and consultant, I wrote many system specifications, mapped and modeled processes. As presales, I had the opportunity to give lots of presentations, build POCs, drive consensus, build strong client relationships with focus on customer service orientation, and finally as a Product Manager, I managed full product lifecycle, since definition of marketing requirements, product vision, strategy and scope definition, passing through development of MVPs, product roadmaps, working with Agile teams, launched the products and led all required post-launch activities, like performance monitoring and product sustaining.

I have a passion for technology and how it can transform complex problems in creative solutions.

I am a results-oriented and an accountable hands-on professional, who is passionate about advanced technology and problem-solving.
