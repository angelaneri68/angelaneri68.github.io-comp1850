The band

Queen was a rock band, formed in London in 1970, and ative with its original components until 1991, when its vocalist and best perfomer ever died.. Its components are:

  • Freddie Mercury (lead vocals and piano)
  • Brian May (lead guitar and vocals)
  • Roger Taylor (drums and vocals)
  • John Deacon (bass guitar)
