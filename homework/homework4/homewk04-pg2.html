GIMP Tutorial

My decision was to create a pencil drawing from a picture. Maybe I will use it instead of a picture on my project, that will be an online resume. So I used this tutorial: Coloring pages.

I needed to follow the steps:

  1. The image was too big so it was resized and cropped
  2. To find edges it was used the filter: Edge Detect -> Neon. Filters parameters needed to be adjusted
  3. The resulted image was dark, so next step was to invert the colors (Colors->Invert)
  4. Then image was converted to black and white, using Colors-> Desaturate
  5. To adjust contrast on lines, it was used Colors->Threshold
  6. After last step of tutorial, image was colorized, for best results.
